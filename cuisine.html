Indian Cuisine

Biryani

Biryani is a popular Indian rice dish known for its fragrant blend of spices, tender meat (such as chicken, mutton, or fish), or vegetables cooked with basmati rice. It is typically layered and cooked together to create a flavorful and aromatic one-pot meal. Add

Butter Chicken

Butter chicken is a classic North Indian dish featuring succulent pieces of chicken cooked in a rich and creamy tomato-based sauce. It is typically seasoned with a combination of aromatic spices and served with naan or rice. Add

Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a crispy and savory South Indian pancake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. It is typically stuffed with a spiced potato filling and served with coconut chutney and sambar (a lentil-based vegetable soup).Add

Italian Cuisine

Pasta Carbonara

Pasta carbonara is a classic Roman dish made with spaghetti or other pasta varieties, tossed in a creamy sauce composed of eggs, grated cheese (usually Parmigiano-Reggiano or Pecorino Romano), pancetta or bacon, and black pepper. It creates a rich and flavorful combination. Add

Lasagna

Lasagna is a layered pasta dish made with wide sheets of pasta, meat or vegetarian filling, cheese (such as mozzarella and ricotta), and tomato sauce. It is baked until the cheese is melted and bubbly, creating a hearty and indulgent meal. Add

Caprese Salad

Caprese salad is a simple and refreshing salad consisting of sliced tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. It is drizzled with olive oil, sprinkled with salt and pepper, and often served as an appetizer or side dish.Add

Japanese Cuisine

Sushi

Sushi is a well-known Japanese dish that features vinegared rice combined with various ingredients such as raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, and sometimes tropical fruits. It is often served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger. Add

Ramen

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle soup dish with Chinese origins. It consists of wheat noodles served in a savory broth, typically flavored with soy sauce, miso, or pork broth. Ramen is commonly topped with sliced pork, green onions, nori (seaweed), and a soft-boiled egg. Add

Udon

Udon is a type of thick wheat noodle commonly used in Japanese cuisine. It is often served in a hot soy-based broth with toppings like green onions, tempura, and kamaboko (fish cake). Udon can also be enjoyed cold with a dipping sauce.Add
